Chairman's Message

We, at Emcure, believe in being a legally compliant and ethical Organisation. Good Corporate Governance is part of our DNA. Emcure expects every Employee to demonstrate his/her commitment in observing and complying with the various Laws, Rules and Regulations of the Country and help the Company in becoming a responsinble corporate citizen. I look forward to your using the system and ensuring that the Company is legally compliant at all times.
